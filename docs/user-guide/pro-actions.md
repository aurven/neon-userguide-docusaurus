# Pro Actions

## What is Pro Actions?

Depending on your organization, you may have access to built-in tools that assist with writing, such as automation options available through **Pro Actions**, a feature in Neon designed to help streamline your content creation process.

Pro Actions allow you to apply pre-configured functions to your content, such as shortening text, translating, summarizing, and more. These tools are especially useful when you want to quickly refine or adapt your content without manually editing every detail. You can apply a single action or chain multiple actions together to support specific editorial workflows.

By integrating Pro Actions into your writing routine, you can boost productivity, maintain consistency, and focus more on storytelling.

## How do I access Pro Actions?

You can access Pro Actions in three main ways:

### Pro Actions Panel
Located at the bottom of the editor, this panel lets you browse and apply all available Pro Actions to your content.

![Pro Actions Panel](/img/userguide-screenshots/image%2068.png)

![Pro Actions Panel Expanded](/img/userguide-screenshots/image%2069.png)

### Text Selection Menu
When you highlight a portion of text in the body of your story, a floating menu appears with quick actions like *Shorten*, *Lengthen*, or *Find Synonyms*. This allows for fast, in-context editing.

![Text Selection Menu](/img/userguide-screenshots/image%2070.png)

### Title and Summary Suggestions
When you're editing the title or summary fields, a floating suggestion bar appears on the left side. It provides suggestions to help you write or improve these sections by offering alternate phrasing or enhancements.

![Title Summary Suggestions](/img/userguide-screenshots/image%2071.png)

## How do I apply Pro Action functions to my story?

To start using ProActions, you can either select a piece of text in your document or click on an action directly from the ProActions toolbar, depending on the function you want to apply

### Text-Based Actions
Some ProActions are designed to work on a specific piece of content. To use them, highlight the sentence or paragraph you want to work with, then find the available actions in the ProActions panel or floating bar. Here are some actions that require selecting text first:
- Apply text style (bold, italic, underline, script)
- Shorten, Lengthen, Rewrite
- Synonyms
- Make list / Make table
- Web search

![Text Based Pro Actions](/img/userguide-screenshots/image%2072.png)

### Direct Actions
Other ProActions can be used by simply clicking them directly in the toolbar
- Generate and suggest headline/summary
- Transcribe audio
- Translate

![Direct Pro Actions](/img/userguide-screenshots/image%2073.png)

### Metadata Actions
For ProActions functions that focus on metadata, you can access and apply them by opening the Object panel in the Binder, for example to generate and insert an SEO title and meta description.

![Metadata Pro Actions](/img/userguide-screenshots/image%2074.png)

## Can I customize or configure Pro Actions to fit my needs?

Pro Actions are usually pre-configured depending on your organization, along with some additional extensions that might be available. While you can't customize them directly, the options you see are tailored to your workspace setup.

If you're interested in exploring additional or customized Pro Actions, we recommend having a discussion within your organization and reaching out to the Neon team to explore what's possible together.

## How do I use Pro Actions effectively in my workflow?

Pro Actions simplifies and enhances your content creation by offering a variety of AI-powered tools that can be seamlessly integrated into your writing process from start to finish. Here are some key features that can be optimized: 

- **Transcribe:** Automatically convert audio content such as interviews or podcasts into text, saving time and effort in transcription.
- **Content Suggestion:** Get creative ideas and alternative phrasing using AI-powered suggestions to improve headlines, summaries, and article content.
- **Content Transformation:** Paraphrase, summarize, or restructure your text dynamically to make your writing clearer, more engaging, and easy to understand.
- **Multilingual Translation:** Translate your content effortlessly into multiple languages using integrated translation services like DeepL, expanding your audience reach.

You can find all these powerful functions conveniently grouped in the Pro Actions panel, making it easy to enhance your content directly within your editing workflow.

## Why is a function not loading or working properly?

If a Pro Action function isn't working as expected, consider the following:

- **Unsupported content or context**: Some actions only work in certain areas or with certain content. Make sure you're using the function in a supported place with valid content selected.
- **OpenAI integration issue**: Certain Pro Actions rely on external services like OpenAI. If there's a problem with the integration, such as API limits or connectivity issues, the action might fail.

If the issue persists, it could be a temporary problem on the platform. Try again later or contact system administrator or contact support.
