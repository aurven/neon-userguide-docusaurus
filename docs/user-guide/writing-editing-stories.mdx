## How do I create a new story?

To create a new story, click the "Add" button in the top navigation bar or use the Quick Link from your dashboard (if added). [Learn how you can add a Quick Link to your dashboard](/docs/user-guide/dashboard#whats-the-best-way-to-arrange-my-dashboard-for-easy-access). 

![Create Story Button](/img/userguide-screenshots/image%2016.png)

A window will appear where you need to enter the story details, such as
the name, description, issue date, folder, and content type.
Make sure to fill in the **Name** and **Workfolder**, as they are mandatory fields.

You can also choose the **Lifecycle** of your story:

- **Daily** — for time-sensitive content that has a short relevance window,
  such as breaking news or daily reports.
- **Persistent** — for evergreen and reusable content that stays relevant
  over time, such as an About Us page or a standing feature article
  that you want to keep and reuse across your site.

![Story Details Form](/img/userguide-screenshots/image%20171.png)

Once the information is complete, you'll be directed to the **Story Editor** page, where you can begin writing and editing your content including adding images, writing a headline, summary, and body text. You can also take advantage of **Neon's premium features**, such as the **Pro Actions** quick menu and **AI Companion**, which can be tailored and enabled for your newsroom upon request to help streamline your workflow.

![Story Editor Interface](/img/userguide-screenshots/image%2018.png)

## How do I edit an existing story?

You can edit an existing story directly from the dashboard by clicking the pen icon on the story card or by clicking the story and selecting the Edit button.

![Edit Story from Dashboard](/img/userguide-screenshots/image%2019.png)

![Edit Story Options](/img/userguide-screenshots/image%2020.png)

You may also navigate to the 'Search' section to locate the story you wish to edit and click 'open'.

![Edit Story from Search](/img/userguide-screenshots/image%2021.png)

Please note that editing is only available for users who have the appropriate permissions.

## How do I add images, media, or other components to my story?

You can add an image that is already uploaded to Neon by opening the Candidate Browser from the binder on the right side of the Story Editor. Then, simply drag the image you want directly into the story.
You can learn how to effectively find the images or stories you are looking for by visiting the Candidate Browser guide [here](/docs/user-guide/neons-binder#candidate-browser).

![Add Image from Candidate Browser](/img/userguide-screenshots/image%20168.png)

Otherwise, if you want to directly add an image from your computer or an external source, you can add content directly from the Story Editor using the toolbar at the top by following these steps: 

### 1. Adding Images

![Insert Image Menu](/img/userguide-screenshots/image%2023.png)

- Click Insert from the editor toolbar, then select the Image or gallery option depending on your needs
- An image placeholder will appear in your story.

- Then, you can either:
    - Click the upload icon to select a file from your computer.
    - Drag and drop an image into the placeholder area, or
    - Paste a URL to import an image from an external source.
    
    ![Image Upload Options](/img/userguide-screenshots/image%2024.png)
    
    ![Image Placeholder](/img/userguide-screenshots/image%2025.png)

### 2. Adding Other Media (e.g., videos, or other embedded medias)

![Insert Embed Menu](/img/userguide-screenshots/image%2026.png)

- From the Insert menu in the toolbar, select the Embed icon.
- A placeholder will be added to your story.
- Paste the link or embed URL of the media you want to include (e.g., a YouTube video or external audio clip).

Once added, you can fill in the caption, credit, and source fields for context and attribution, position your media within the story, click on the media block, and use the up or down arrows to move it to the desired location in the content.

### 3. Adding Components with Slash "/" Menu
In Neon, you can also use the **Slash Menu** by typing `/` in the editor
to bring up a list of components you can insert, then type the name
of the component you want to add, such as text, image, or table.

 ![Image Placeholder](/img/userguide-screenshots/image%20172.png)


You can also add a component by clicking the **+** icon on the left
side of any paragraph to access the same options.
 ![Image Placeholder](/img/userguide-screenshots/image%20173.png)



## How do I edit or crop an image to fit my story layout?

To edit or crop an image in your story, you can follow these steps:

- Click **pen icon** or **edit button** on the image you want to edit or crop
    
    ![Edit Image Button](/img/userguide-screenshots/image%2027.png)
    
- Choose the desired aspect ratio
    
    ![Aspect Ratio Options](/img/userguide-screenshots/image%2028.png)
    
    You can also add a custom aspect ratio based on your needs by clicking the **+** icon and entering your custom crop name along with the desired width and height.
    
    ![Custom Aspect Ratio](/img/userguide-screenshots/image%2029.png)
    
- Adjust the image by dragging it so that the important area fits within the frame.
    - Make sure there are no red borders, as these indicate that parts of the frame are not fully covered by the image.
- You can also use the **Auto Zoom** feature to automatically adjust the image to fit the selected ratio, or enable the **Show Focal Point** option to highlight the most important area of the image during cropping.
    
    ![Image Cropping Interface](/img/userguide-screenshots/image%2030.png)
    
- When you're done, click **Save**.

## How do I link another article to my story?

You can add a link to another story by opening the Candidate Browser from the binder on the right side of the Story Editor. Then, simply drag the story you want to add into your current story. This helps you link related articles, providing additional context and guiding your readers to explore more relevant content.

![Link Another Article](/img/userguide-screenshots/image%2031.png)

## How can I preview how my story will look when published?

To preview your story, open the Object Panel from the Binder. After selecting a publication site, click the Show Preview icon. 

![Preview Story](/img/userguide-screenshots/image%2034.png)

A new page will open, showing how your story will appear once published. At the top, you'll see a bar indicating the visibility time if a specific date and time have been set. This bar also allows you to navigate to the live site, view different versions, and access the story's properties.

![Story Preview](/img/userguide-screenshots/image%2035.png)

## How can I structure or format my story for better readability?

To improve the readability of your story, here are some tips that you can follow:

- Use headings and subheadings to organize your content and guide the reader through different sections.
- Break text into short paragraphs to make it easier to scan and digest.
- Use bullet points or numbered lists to present information clearly and efficiently.
- Highlight important points with bold or italic formatting, but use them selectively to avoid visual clutter.
- Add relevant images or embeds to make the story more engaging and support your content visually.
- Avoid overly complex sentences and aim for clear, direct language.
- Use white space to give your layout a clean and breathable look.

You can always fix formatting using the top menu bar or improve sentences using the automation features available in Pro Actions (see the Pro Actions section to learn more). Furthermore, you can preview your story regularly to check the layout and readability before publishing.

## How can I use Neon's features to speed up the writing and editing process?

Neon offers built-in tools like **Pro Actions** and the **AI Companion** to help streamline your writing and editing workflow. These features let you automate tasks like translating content, shortening text, suggesting headlines, and performing quick web searches. You can also use the AI Companion to brainstorm ideas and write directly within the editor.

Some of these capabilities are part of **AI Premium Features,** advanced tools that may not be available to all users yet but will be available for purchase in the future.

If you don't have access to these features yet but want to learn more, see the Pro Actions and AI Companion sections. You can also contact the Neon team to explore upcoming AI Premium Features or request access.

## Why can't I edit a story?

If you're unable to edit a story, here are a few things to check:

- **Currently being edited by another user**: The story is currently open in a private editing session by another user. To ensure content consistency and prevent conflicts, only one person can edit a story at a time. This dedicated session helps protect your changes and ensures a smooth, uninterrupted editing experience.
- **Permission restrictions**: Your role may not grant you editing access to this story. Please check your permissions or contact your administrator if you believe you should have editing rights.

## What should I do if I accidentally close my story without saving?

Neon automatically saves your work **locally** every 15 seconds while you're editing, helping reduce the risk of losing progress during your session. To ensure your content is permanently saved and accessible across devices or sessions, click the **diskette icon** in the top-left corner or press **CTRL+S** to store it in the database.

![Save Story](/img/userguide-screenshots/image%2036.png)

If you need to revisit or recover previous versions of your story, you can access the **Version List** from the **Binder** on the right panel of the Story Editor. This lets you view and restore past saved versions, helping you stay in control even when something unexpected happens.

![Version History](/img/userguide-screenshots/image%2037.png)
