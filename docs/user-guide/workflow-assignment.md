# Workflow and Assignment
## How do I move story to next stage assign a story to my colleague?

When you're in the story editor, click the **Workflow panel**
![Workflow in Story Editor](/img/userguide-screenshots/image%20158.png)


In this panel you can:
- Select the **workflow status** of your story
- Find and assign **users** to the story
- Assign the story **to yourself**
- Add a **priority**, **due date**, or **comment**

![Workflow Panel](/img/userguide-screenshots/image%20159.png)


You can also access the Workflow panel by clicking your story from **Faceted Search** and selecting Workflow.

![Faceted Search Workflow](/img/userguide-screenshots/image%20160.png)

![Faceted Search Workflow](/img/userguide-screenshots/image%20161.png)


---

## Where can I find stories assigned to me?

You can find stories assigned to you by going to **Faceted Search** and using the **Assigned To** filter in the left panel. You can also add additional filters such as **Priority** or **Workflow Status** to narrow down your results. Similarly, use the **Assigned By** filter to find stories you've assigned to others.

![Faceted Search Workflow](/img/userguide-screenshots/image%20162.png)


Once you have your search results, you can **save the query** so you don't need to re-enter the filters every time you search for your assignments. Check how to save a query [here](./faceted-search#how-do-i-save-a-search-query). After saving a query, you can also **add it to your dashboard** for quick access to your latest assignments. Find the guide [here](./dashboard).




## How can I review changes made by others?

To review changes, open your Story, Liveblog, or WebPage, then click the **Binder** icon and go to the **Versions** section. There, you can see a full history of edits made, both by you and by anyone else who has worked on the content.

![Version History](/img/userguide-screenshots/image%20117.png)
